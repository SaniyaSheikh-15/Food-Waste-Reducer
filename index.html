How It Works

1. Donate Food

Restaurants and citizens add surplus food donations with details about quantity, type, and pickup location.

2. NGOs Claim

NGOs browse available donations and claim items that match their distribution needs.

3. Driver Pickup

Volunteer drivers assign themselves to deliveries and transport food from donors to NGOs.

4. Impact Created

Food reaches those in need, reducing waste and creating positive community impact.
